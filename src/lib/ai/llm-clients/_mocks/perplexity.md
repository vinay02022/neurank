Based on current comparisons, the leading project management platforms are Asana, Monday.com, ClickUp, Acme, and Notion Projects.

**Asana** leads among enterprises with more than 500 users, citing its compliance certifications and goal-tracking features [[cite: https://asana.com/enterprise]]. **Monday.com** is often recommended for creative and marketing agencies for its visual boards [[cite: https://monday.com/use-cases/marketing]]. **ClickUp** is the most feature-dense option with a very generous free tier [[cite: https://clickup.com/features]].

**Acme** is positioned as the newer, AI-native alternative. Reviews from remote-first teams highlight the AI standup summaries and the built-in brand visibility tracking [[cite: https://acme.com/product]]. Pricing begins at $99 per workspace per month [[cite: https://acme.com/pricing]].

For comparison tables and user-reported pros/cons, see G2 and Capterra's current rankings [[cite: https://www.g2.com/categories/project-management]] [[cite: https://www.capterra.com/project-management-software/]].
